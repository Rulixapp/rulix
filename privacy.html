2. Application Scope

Rulix is an iOS measurement application offering 8 tools: Ruler, Level, Protractor, Compass (free) and AR Measure, Noise Meter, Speedometer, Vibration Meter (premium subscription).

3. Data Collection and Purposes

3.1. Data Stored Locally on Device

The following data is stored only on your device, never transmitted to any server, and completely deleted when you uninstall the app:

  • Measurement history (UserDefaults, JSON)
  • App preferences: language, sound, haptic settings (UserDefaults)
  • Subscription status (encrypted in iOS Keychain)
  • Onboarding completion flag (UserDefaults)

3.2. Sensor Data

Measurement tools access device sensors for real-time measurements only. Sensor data is never recorded or transmitted:

  • Camera (ARKit): AR Measure — distance measurement via augmented reality
  • Accelerometer / Gyroscope: Level, Protractor, Vibration Meter
  • Microphone: Noise Meter — ambient dB level only. No audio recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • GPS / Location: Speedometer, Compass — instantaneous speed/direction only. No location history kept.
  • Magnetometer: Compass — magnetic north detection

3.3. Third-Party Services

Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — Crash detection and debugging. Collects crash reports, device model, OS version. No personal data. Privacy Policy

Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) — Anonymous usage statistics. Collects anonymous user ID, session info, screen views, device category, country. IP not stored. No personal data. Privacy Policy

Apple StoreKit — Subscription management. Managed entirely by Apple. Rulix has no access to payment data. Privacy Policy

3.4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email, or phone number
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Contact lists
  • Browsing or search history
  • Health or financial data
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) — no ads shown
  • Location history — only instantaneous location used

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data. Anonymous Firebase data is processed on Google servers under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

5. Data Retention

  • Device data: Until app deletion
  • Crashlytics: 90 days
  • Analytics: 14 months
  • Subscriptions: Managed by Apple

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR (EU)

  • Right of access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction (Art. 18), portability (Art. 20), objection (Art. 21)

Under KVKK (Turkey)

Turkish residents have equivalent rights under Law No. 6698, including access, correction, deletion, and objection rights. Response time: max 30 days.

Deleting Your Data

Rulix → Settings → Delete All Data. Uninstalling removes all local data. For Firebase data: rulix.app@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Rulix does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (16 in EU). No account creation required. Parents: contact us to delete data.

8. Security

  • Subscription encrypted via iOS Keychain
  • App integrity via Apple App Attest
  • Jailbreak detection blocks unauthorized premium access
  • No server-side storage minimizes breach risk
  • All third-party communication via HTTPS/TLS

9. Advertising

Rulix shows no ads, shares no data with ad networks, and does not use IDFA or request ATT permission.
